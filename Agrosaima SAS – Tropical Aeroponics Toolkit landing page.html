About Us

Agrosaima is a Colombian AgriFoodTech company with 10+ years of R&D. We developed a production toolkit for the tropical highlands combining: tropical aeroponics (horizontal), climate‑adapted macro‑tunnels, rainwater‑only irrigation, and solar‑powered pumping & ventilation. Output: premium basil, herbs and microgreens with export‑grade consistency, safety and nutritional density.

Export Climate‑Smart Rain‑Fed Solar‑Powered Traceable

Value Proposition

  • Consistent quality, quantity & compliance → export contracts.
  • Nutrient‑dense, pesticide‑free fresh produce.
  • Area efficiency comparable to NL greenhouses — without vertical farming.
  • Modular replication across Colombia’s coffee region.
  • Water: >90% savings vs. soil (rainwater only).
  • Energy: solar pumping & ventilation → near‑zero OPEX.
  • Traceability: export‑ready protocols (GlobalG.A.P./USDA Organic in process).
  • ESG: rural skilled jobs, conservation buffers.

Export Focus

Target markets: USA (NY, FL, CA) and EU (NL, DE, ES). Premium importers of fresh basil, herbs and microgreens.

Indicative FOB ranges: Basil US$8–12/kg · Microgreens US$25–40/kg · Mixed herbs US$10–15/kg.

  • Colombia (Agrosaima): rain + solar, low OPEX, replicable horizontal system.
  • Netherlands: high‑tech vertical greenhouses → higher CAPEX/OPEX.
  • Mexico: export leader with increasing water stress and pesticide restrictions.
